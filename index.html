Privacy Policy for RideTrack

Last Updated: December 2024

Welcome to RideTrack! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

RideTrack collects the following information to provide our services:

  • Location Data: Precise GPS location for real-time vehicle tracking
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the app for improvement purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time vehicle tracking services
  • Display your location accurately on maps
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix technical issues and bugs

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is handled with care:

  • Location data is processed in real-time during active tracking sessions
  • We do not permanently store your location history
  • Data transmission is secured using HTTPS encryption
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps Platform: For map display and location services. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Expo: For app development and deployment tools.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • You can disable location services in your device settings at any time
  • You can uninstall the app to stop all data collection
  • Location tracking only occurs when the app is actively in use

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.
